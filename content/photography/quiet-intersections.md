Walking through the small, low-traffic alleys of Tokyo during early autumn. In the middle of the world's most populated metropolis, there are pockets of absolute stillness. 

A single red bicycle resting against an age-old concrete fence. A soft lamp glow blending into the amber dusk. Recording these quiet corners allows preserving an oasis of sanity inside the daily hum. Shot on Sony A7III, 50mm F/1.8, 1/200s at ISO800.
