After months of late-night DSP experiments and structural optimizations in Node.js and C++, we finally pushed the button on the production infrastructure. 

Seeing the connection times immediately drop, hearing the crystal clear audio, and watching the canvases sync instantly across hundreds of concurrent, multi-user web environments was a deeply rewarding moment. Building robust systems isn't just theory; it's the thrill of live validation.
