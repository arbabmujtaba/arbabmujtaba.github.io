"Weniger, aber besser." Dieter Rams' core principle is the ultimate design philosophy for software engineering. Shunning frivolous animations, bloated feature-sets, and excess clutter leaves room for absolute focus on key operations. 

In this portfolio website, each spacing, typeface, and border is designed deliberately to create a highly aesthetic and composed digital lab.
