Released in 1992. Richard D. James produced these timeless electronic compositions using modified analog gear in his bedroom. 

The album has a high-concept mechanical, space-oriented, and serene texture that perfectly coordinates with writing dense system algorithms, debugging compilers, or editing photos deep into the night. It is a masterpiece that sounds as forward-thinking today as it did decades ago.
